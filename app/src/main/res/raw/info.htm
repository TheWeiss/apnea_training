Practice holding your breath. CO2 and O2 tables

Highly often people who have completed a training course freediving, ask how You can train your breath hold home-away-from sea to better prepare for the next trip and to not lose shape.

pretty effective and popular among freedivers training, allowing significantly increase the length of breath, is a static delay training on CO2 and O2 tables. What is it and why it is necessary?

Table CO2. Hypercapnia

How well known, the main trigger level is to inhale carbon dioxide in the lungs. With breath-hold and increase the amount of carbon dioxide, our brain sends a signal respiratory system to breathe. Thus breath holding divided into 2 phases: phase comfort and phase of the struggle, when the brain actively sends signals to the respiratory system, and that begins answer that diaphragm contraction (contractions).

Training is a series of respiratory delays aimed at the development of the organism tolerance to higher CO2 levels (Hypercapnia), increasing carbon dioxide levels during each following the delay. For this purpose, the delay time is breathing constant, and the time of rest between latency gradually decreases, thereby increasing the residual level of CO2 for each following the delay.

Recommended select delay time between 50% and 80% of its maximum. The start time of rest for 2-3 minutes. Reduce the time interval rest 15-20 seconds.

Table O2. Hypoxic table

Series breathing delay, aimed at developing the body's tolerance to a decrease in oxygen level (hypoxia). This is achieved by a gradual increasing the duration of the delay of breath at the same time recreation. rest interval should be large enough to catch to get rid of excessive levels of CO2, accumulated after the delay, and recover. Delay interval large enough to receive hypoxic required load, i.e. by the end of the table delay must be close to maximum.

Remember that all independent training on respiratory arrest spending only on land! The first rule of freediving - never dive alone, refers including to the static delay breathing.

For convenience, we recommend that you use the clip to the nose, and Tracking and locking to acquire results oximeter. You can create a table in which to record important parameters such as heart rate, blood oxygen saturation oxygen, the starting time of contraction, and the maximum delay time etc. This will analyze your results, and see progress It will create an additional incentive for training.

At breath at the time one of the important principles - not to think about time. Therefore it is recommended to practice the tables use special applications for your phone, which configure the necessary parameters of training, after which you comfortably located on the bed, put next to phone and the app tells you all the instructions.
